LEAGUE

For all those interested in joining our Bubble Soccer League please send an email to BSL@atbubblesoccer.ca. We will begin the league as we generate more interest.

BUBBLE SOCCER RULES

  • 1)NO OUTDOOR CLEATS ALLOWED
    • Indoor soccer shoes and running shoes must be worn at all times
    • All games are 40 minutes long, with two 20 minutes halves
    • There will be a 2 minute intermission between the first and second half
  • 2)NO HITTING FROM BEHIND
    • If a player hits an opposing player from behind it is an automatic penalty shot.
    • If a player or team continually hits players from behind, the referee is able to eject said player(s) from the game.
  • 3)DON'T HIT PLAYERS ON THE GROUND
    • Hitting a player on the ground is a 2 minute penalty.
  • 4)NO HITTING AFTER THE WHISTLE
    • Hitting after the whistle is not allowed. Hitting any player after the whistle is a 2 minute penalty.
  • 5)NO SLIDE TACKLING
    • Slide tackling, kicking other players, and head first dives are not allowed.
    • If a player slide tackles another, he or she will be given a two minute penalty.
    • Kicking other players is an automatic ejection and suspension, followed by a 5 minute penalty.
  • 6)STOPPAGE IN PLAY
    • If a player sits on the soccer ball, play will be stopped and the ball will be awarded to the other team.
    • If the soccer ball lands inside a players bubble, play will be stopped and there will be a drop ball.
  • 7)SUBSTITUTIONS
    • Teams are only allowed to substitute players when play is stopped
    • Substitution can not be made when there is less than 3 minutes to play in each half

TEAM RULES

  • Teams can have a maximum of 12 players on their roster
  • All players must be registered and have signed a waiver to play.
  • Teams will play 4v4.
  • Teams must have a minimum of 3 players on the field at all times.
  • A team with less than 3 players on game day will be given an automatic forfeit.
